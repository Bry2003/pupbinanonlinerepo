IntelHub is the official online research repository of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Biñan Campus. This platform serves as your gateway to a rich world of academic discovery, innovation, and collaboration.

Designed to preserve and promote the scholarly output of our community, IntelHub provides access to undergraduate theses, capstone projects, faculty research, and more. Whether you are a student, researcher, or curious learner, this platform empowers you to explore and contribute to the body of knowledge.

Our Mission
  • Centralizing scholarly and creative works of the PUP Biñan community.
  • Promoting Open Access to support learning and knowledge sharing.
  • Fostering Collaboration among scholars, educators, and learners.
  • Supporting Lifelong Learning with a reliable, accessible digital platform.
