About Us

IntelHub is the official online research repository of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Biñan Campus, created to serve as a centralized hub for the collection, preservation, and dissemination of scholarly and creative works produced by students, faculty, and researchers within the campus. Our platform is designed to uphold academic excellence and promote open access to knowledge, reflecting PUP Biñan’s commitment to intellectual growth, innovation, and community empowerment.

At IntelHub, we believe that research should not remain hidden within the walls of the university. Instead, it should be accessible, discoverable, and impactful. That’s why IntelHub provides a digital space where undergraduate theses, capstone projects, faculty research, technical papers, case studies, and digital learning materials are archived and made available to a wider audience. By doing so, we not only support academic collaboration and lifelong learning but also contribute to solving real-world problems through informed insights and evidence-based knowledge.

Our repository is more than just a collection of documents—it is a living archive that evolves with each academic year, capturing the ideas, innovations, and achievements of the PUP Biñan community. Whether you are a student seeking inspiration, a researcher looking for credible sources, or a faculty member aiming to showcase your work, IntelHub is here to support your journey.

IntelHub is managed by a dedicated team committed to ensuring quality, accessibility, and continuous improvement of our digital library. Through this initiative, we aim to foster a research-oriented culture within the university and contribute meaningfully to the global academic landscape.
